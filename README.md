# About Me 
##     Who I am

I am Lucas Brookshire, a senior in high school who's main passion is game development. I have always thought AI would be impossible when I was younger, but look where we are now. AI is here and it's become a tool that can make insane projects, help people learn, and it gets better each day.  The thing that fascinates me about AI is how much the functions work like a child's brain, it's cool to look at it and relate it to humans and learn from it. 

I want AI to help in education, in the backend things that few people like compared to the font end, and doing paperwork that is typically a hassle for humans to do. AI can help expand a human's research scale, as it can bring different sources and bring them together for you. These types of things can help students learn more and give teachers another opinion. I also want AI to handle the things that humans find typically annoying, this way humans can have more fun and interest in their projects. Let's look at game development for example, people love to watch the program work and their projects come to life with the art they made. They don't want to deal with the paperwork to become a business, or the expenses of publishing, if AI can handle those parts, more people could do game development and not worry about those types of things. 

My philosophy on AI is that as long as the project itself is mostly your work, that is A-OK. You can use AI to improve and double check your work, and have it do the boring paperwork for you. It is still your project, but AI has given you a hand in narrowing things down, giving you new information, it could be anything. 

## What Drives Me

I plan to make people's lives more interesting, and keep enjoying their projects without the crazy paperwork. I tend to never finish any project I start, and it is always that backend that gets me, if that was erased, I would love to see what I could do. I want to see AI to the paperwork and help teach others so that there is no excuse for not finishing something.
